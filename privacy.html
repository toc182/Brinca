Privacy Policy — Brinca

Effective date: April 2026

1. Who we are

Brinca is operated from Panama. For questions about this policy, contact privacy@brinca.app.

2. What this app does

Brinca is a tool for parents, therapists, coaches, and other adult caregivers to track structured practice sessions with children they are responsible for. The app is used by adults. Children do not use the app directly.

3. Information we collect

We collect:

  • Account information: email, password (stored encrypted), display name, role description (parent, therapist, coach, teacher, other), profile photo (optional).
  • Information about children you add: name, date of birth, gender, country of residence, grade level, optional profile photo, optional weight and height history, optional external-activity descriptions.
  • Activity and session information: activity names, drill definitions, counts, timer values, checklist results, free-text notes, and optional photos.
  • Technical information: crash reports (via Sentry), subscription status (via RevenueCat). These contain a random app-generated identifier, not your name or email.

We do not collect location, contacts, microphone audio outside the Voice Note feature, advertising identifiers, or behavioral analytics.

4. How we use this information

  • To provide the core features of the app (tracking sessions, showing progress, managing rewards).
  • To authenticate your account.
  • To diagnose crashes and errors.
  • To manage your subscription (if you purchase one).

We do not use your information to show advertisements, and we do not sell your information.

5. Who we share information with

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider.
  • Sentry — receives crash reports.
  • RevenueCat — receives subscription state.
  • Apple — processes payments for subscriptions.
  • Family members you invite — anyone you add to your family can see shared children's profiles and session data, according to the role you assigned them.

We do not sell personal information to anyone.

6. Data retention

  • Account and child information is retained while your account is active.
  • When you delete your account, all personal data is deleted from active systems within 30 days. Encrypted backups may retain data for up to 90 days.
  • Crash reports are retained by Sentry for 90 days.

7. Your rights

Under Panamanian law (Ley 81 of 2019), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You may delete your account directly from Settings > Accounts Center > Delete account.

8. Children

Brinca is designed for adult caregivers. Children do not create accounts and do not use the app directly. Information about children is entered by the caregiver, who confirms during sign-up that they are the child's parent or legal guardian, or that they have permission.

9. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy materially, we will notify you inside the app and require you to accept the new version before continuing.
